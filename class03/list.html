Chicken Biryani

Biryani is my favorite food. It is a rice dish with spiced chicken and herbs. This recipe is easy for beginners. No fancy cooking skills needed.



"A plate of homemade Chicken Biryani"



Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (thighs or drumsticks)
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 large onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 cup coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup mint leaves (chopped)
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • Pinch of saffron or food color (optional)

Cooking Steps

  1. Marinate chicken. Mix chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, and salt. Let it sit for 15 minutes.
  2. Boil rice. In a pot, boil 4 cups of water with salt. Add rinsed rice and cook until rice is 70 percent done. Drain and set aside.
  3. Fry onions. Heat oil in a large pot. Fry sliced onions until golden brown. Remove half of the onions for garnish later.
  4. Cook chicken. In the same pot, add marinated chicken, green chilies, and chopped tomatoes. Cook for 5 to 7 minutes until chicken changes color.
  5. First layer of rice. Spread half of the cooked rice over the chicken mixture.
  6. Add herbs. Sprinkle chopped coriander, mint leaves, and half of the fried onions over the rice.
  7. Second layer of rice. Add the remaining rice on top. Sprinkle the rest of the fried onions and saffron water if using.
  8. Dum cooking. Cover the pot with a tight lid. Cook on very low heat for 20 minutes. Do not open the lid.
  9. Rest and serve. Turn off the heat. Wait 5 minutes. Gently mix and serve hot.

Extra Toppings and Side Items

These sides make biryani even better:

  • Raita (yogurt dip)
    • Cucumber raita
    • Onion raita
    • Mint raita
  • Fresh salad
    • Onion rings
    • Lemon wedges
    • Green chilies
  • Extra toppings for garnish
    • Fried cashews
    • Boiled egg slices
    • Extra fried onions
    • Lemon juice
